Help Build the Future of Liberty Hill Football & Cheer

We’re raising funds to ensure every kid can participate, strengthen our program with safer training equipment, support cheer competition fees, and celebrate our athletes with end-of-season awards.

Liberty Hill Youth Football & Cheer (LHYFC) is a nonprofit, 501(c)(3) volunteer-run organization located in Liberty Hill, Texas. It is designed to foster community, sportsmanship, and teamwork among youth athletes, with operations supported by volunteers and tax-exempt status as an educational/amateur sports institution.
